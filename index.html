Eastbourne Warriors

MMA Gym in Eastbourne East Sussex

Welcome to Eastbourne Warriors! We are a MMA Gym offering training in a variety of martial arts including: Boxing, Kickboxing, Muay Thai, Judo, BJJ and Wrestling. MMA is currently one of the fastest growing sports in the world, and is a great way to get fit, while having fun, and making new friends. We have members who compete in the UFC, others train for the self-defence aspect, some just want to learn martial arts, and others train for the fitness and fun side. Whether you are training with the aim of competing in the UFC, or want to learn some self defense, or just want to get fit, we can help you reach your desired goals.

Striking Arts

Boxing

Boxing uses a 2 point striking system (your fists) to inflict maximum damage to your opponent. Although using your fists is a key part in boxing, good footwork (which is often overlooked) is also crucial to becoming a proficient boxer. All our training is done to a high standard, and is done in a safe controled environment, and we won't push you to do anything you aren't comfortable with.

Kickboxing

Kickboxing is a 4 point strike system (your fists and feet) to inflict damage to your opponent. The stance in kickboxing is similar, but different to Boxing and Muay-Thai's stances, and the kicks are slightly different to Muay-Thai, making it a highly effective self-defence martial-art.

Muay Thai

Muay-Thai is known as the art of 8 limbs for a reason, it uses an 8 point striking system (your fists, shins, knees and elbows) and is reknowned world wide as being one of the most, if not the most, devastating martial arts. First formed from wars in Thailand, several hundred years ago, it is highly practical self defense, due to its many striking options.
