Eastbourne Warriors

MMA Gym in Eastbourne East Sussex

Welcome to Eastbourne Warriors! We are a MMA Gym offering training in a variety of martial arts including: Boxing, Kickboxing, Muay Thai, Judo, BJJ and Wrestling. MMA is currently one of the fastest growing sports in the world, and is a great way to get fit, while having fun, and making new friends. We have members who compete in the UFC, others train for the self-defence aspect, some just want to learn martial arts, and others train for the fitness and fun side. Whether you are training with the aim of competing in the UFC, or want to learn some self defense, or just want to get fit, we can help you reach your desired goals.
